Some of my photos are covered in foreign publications

Recently, one of my portrait works was printed in a well-known English magazine Digital SLR Photography

This photo was shot on the embankment of Istanbul In the middle of March. Usually the weather at this time of year is already warm and pleases with the first spring rays of the sun, but that day turned out to be cold and windy. The strongest gusts of wind were close to the sea area, where, because of the high humidity, the cold felt stronger.

We were passing the ancient destroyed wall of the fortress in search of an interesting one-off background, for a moment a bright ray of light cut through the cloud and we stopped. Because of the wind, tears appeared in the model eyes, they glittered with bright light and this gave dramatic effect to this photo.
